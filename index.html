Local Specialties

Choco Buns

Our classic hawaiian sweet roll made with sustainable cocoa, and filled with a coconut mousse will have your tastebuds on Island-time

Cinnamon Rolls

Bubbling with sweet and comforting cinnamon sugar, drizzled with cream cheese whipped frosting, caramel and Belgian chocolate sauce (with recomended optionals like pecans and graham crackers) this sweet and gooey pleasure is almost too pure for this world!...Almost.

Honey Bites

Charming and simple, our world famous funnel cake batter takes on a cute little twist by bee-ing dunked in organic honey, and sprinkled with your choice of toppings! all proceeds go toward saving the bees and gorilla gardening efforts across Canada.
